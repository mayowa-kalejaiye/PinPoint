Privacy Notice

What we collect

How we use it

  • Show live driver progress and ETA
  • Help the driver find the exact spot you selected
  • Improve reliability and prevent abuse of the service

How long we keep it

  • Session data clears when you close the browser

  • We do not store payment information

Your controls

  • You can clear session data inside the app
  • You can request deletion of delivery-related data after completion
